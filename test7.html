Elephant Ride In Thekkady

Elephant ride in Thekkady is one of the most popular activity for tourists in the city. A Thekkady elephant ride offers a chance to enjoy a breathtaking view of the tea and cardamom plantations to tourists. While on an elephant, you can access the inaccessible areas of the Periyar National Park.

Banana Ride In Kochi

Kerala, being a popular holiday destination with serene backwaters and beaches, is a hotspot for water sports. Depending on the terrain of the location, the things to do in Kerala vary from city to city. If you want to enjoy a banana ride in Kochi, then you are in absolute luck!

Jeep Safari, Thekkady

Jeep safari in Thekkady through the rocky terrains is a thrilling experience.The safari covers the dense and lush forests, waterfalls, spice plantations, and the rich green vegetation. It is a popular alternative to hiking. The activity takes place every day from 6 in the morning to 5 in the evening.

Marine Drive In Kochi

The Marine Drive in Kochi is as picturesque and as beautifully landscaped as the Marine Drive in Mumbai.There is a chill vibe to the place which attracts people to that spot. The backwaters backdrop and the shore lined with anchored ships creates a perfect scenery.

Mangaladevi Temple In Thekkady

Mangaladevi Temple in Thekkady is a 1000 years old temple which is located in the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. This temple is located at a distance of 15 kilometers from Thekkady. Mangaladevi Temple is situated at an altitude of about 1300 metres above the sea level.

Karumadi Kuttan In Alleppey

Karumadi Kuttan in Alleppey is a well-known Buddhist pilgrim center. The 11th-century statue of Lord Buddha has fascinating legends associated with it.Karumadi Kuttan is accredited as someone who possessed healing powers, so the locals regard him with affection and respect.

Itinerary

Day 1: Welcome To Goa
Sightseeing
Leisure Day

The first day of your Goa honeymoon itinerary welcomes you to the scintillating land of Goa. Your journey begins.

Upon arrival at Goa airport/railway station, an agent’s representative will pick you up and escort you to the hotel. Register yourself at the hotel’s reception and relax for a while. Enjoy the first day of your romantic trip leisurely. Enjoy a long walk with your partner at the sandy beaches of Goa or experience the vibrant nightlife on your Goa honeymoon.

Towards the end of the day, come back to the hotel and stay for the night.

Day 2: North Goa sightseeing
Dolphin Spotting
Calangute Beach
Baga Beach

This day, embark on a North Goa sightseeing tour and experience the best part of your Goa holiday.

Wake up early and head out together for a fun-filled dolphin sightseeing tour. Watch dolphins dancing in the sea waves. Come back to the hotel and have breakfast. Later, set out for North Goa sightseeing tour. Explore Coco Beach, Calangute Annexe, Calangute Beach, Baga Beach, and Anjuna Beach with this Goa tour package for couples. or experience the vibrant nightlife on your Goa honeymoon.

Visit the sightseeing attractions of Old Goa, such as Basilica of Bom Jesus and Se Cathedral, dating back to the Portuguese Era. Spend some quality time together at the Dona Paula Bay while enjoying mesmerising views of the Panjim city. Sip a cup of hot coffee at any of the beach shacks at Miramar Beach with your beloved.

In the evening, come back to the hotel and rest. followed by a restful sleep.

Day 3: South Goa sightseeing
Miramar Beach
Dona paula bay

Witness the calm and serene South Goa with your better half on your honeymoon in Goa

Kickstart the day with a nutritious breakfast and head out for a romantic sightseeing tour of South Goa. The first attraction to be visited on the third day of your Goa itinerary is the Shri Shantadurga Temple at Kavlem. Also, seek blessings for your newly-wed life at Shri Manguesh Temple in Priol.

Towards the end of the day, come back to the hotel and stay for the night.

Day 4: Enjoy Leisure Day
Shopping
Unlimited Fun

The fourth day of your Goa honeymoon trip is entirely at your disposal.

Wake up as per your convenience and savour a healthy breakfast. Get cosy with your soulmate in your hotel room or go out and indulge in shopping at any of the flea markets in Goa. Bid goodbye to the day with a peaceful sleep at the hotel.

Day 5: Departure back home
Departure

Depart from Goa with a treasure trove of memories.

Post breakfast, check-out from the hotel and get transferred to the airport/railway station. From here, board your flight/train back home.
